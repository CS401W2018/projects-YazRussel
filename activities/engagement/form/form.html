  2. The form tag has a "method" attribute. What does it do?
    The method attribute let you choose between get and post. Keep in mind that this two are secured.
  3. What is the difference between a "POST" and a "GET"?
    When we do the method get all of the information goes to the http protocol on the top of the website. When we are reading information, to request data from a specified source.
    The method post changes information, this will also hide your query string on the search tool on the website. Use this when you want to send a data to a server to create or update a resource. The post method is just a little bit secured because it doesn't show on your address bar.
