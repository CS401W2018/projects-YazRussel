We will discuss the following:

  • Structuural Tags (HEADER, NAV, MAIN, FOOTER)
  • What goes in the header?
    • The header tag is on the top part of every web. It will and should stay the same on every page.
  • What goes in the Nav? Can the Nav go in the header? Does it have to go in the header? Does the Nav contain links to another website?
    • Yes, navigation can go to the header, but not necessarily. They are doing AB testing, to find what web layout does the user visit more. The navigation, has a design when the cursor point or click the navigation. The navigation usually have a link connected to it. The link will direct you to a different webpage when clicked.
  • What goes in Main?
    • Where's you main content goes into. Main tag will change when you go to a different navigation.
  • What goes in the Footer?
    • What they usually put in the footer is the copyright, privacy notice (a policy that a user should follow), leagal notices, cookies (this collects data), and some useful links.
