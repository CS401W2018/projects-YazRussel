Recent Projects

Astral Assault

Astral Assault is an engaging sliding puzzle game that features a celestial duel between the forces of the Sun and Moon. Players take turns pressing a star button to alternately place their tokens — either a sun or a moon — on a dynamic game board. The objective is to strategically form a line of five tokens in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally, amidst the ever-shifting tiles of the game board.

Maze Runner

Maze Runner is a thrilling strategy game that challenges players to navigate the twists and turns of a complex labyrinth to collect essential items needed to triumph over a formidable opponent. Inspired by the biblical story of David and Goliath, the game pits the quick-witted David against the giant Goliath in a test of cunning and courage.

Github Repo

Pokemon Game

Pokemon Game is an interactive game where players need to quickly identify and select Pokémon based on their position in a grid. The goal is to clear the board by correctly selecting Pokémon in sequence according to their designated numbers.
