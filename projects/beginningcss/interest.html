Interest - Mobile Legends

Interest Hero

This is a hobby I've played for a few years now. It's a mobile game that allows you to interact with other players and plan strategies to defeat the opposing team. It all started two years after I returned from my mission. I noticed that my nephews and younger brother were always playing this game, and they would even invite me to join, but I always said no. Eventually, I noticed they were developing bad habits from playing, such as frustration, poor manners, and even trash talking. I was disappointed, so I told them to stop playing, but it was too lateâ€”they were already addicted.

So, I decided to start playing the game myself to better understand how I could help them. I taught them how to keep their composure when they were angry, how to communicate effectively with their team, how to appreciate their teammates when they did something right, and, most importantly, that the game is meant for entertainment and should not define their lives.

Overall, I enjoy this game because of my family. It's my way of bonding with my younger brother and nephews, while also using a modern approach to teach them good manners. I continue to play this game to relieve stress and make new friends online.
