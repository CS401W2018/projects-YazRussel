Hero - Wise

Intrest Hero

James Del Rosario or also known in his in-game-name as Wise, have been playing Mobile Legend for a few years now, and have joined the several tournaments in southeast asia, and over the world winning the trophy. Wise plays the assasin role, which is the hardest role in the game that made him gained popularity in the image of Mobile Legends.

I started watching his game to improve my gameplay, as I also noticed that he is always calm and reserve whenever he play. He serve as an assistant to the captain in his team, where he is in charge of map awareness or giving intel to his team. He is great at roaming around in the enemies jungle and that gives his team a great advantage.
