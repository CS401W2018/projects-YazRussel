Sinigang

The recipe for ultimate comfort food is sinigang na baboy! Hearty and tasty on its own or eaten with steamed rice, it's made with pork ribs, veggies, and a broth flavored with tamarind. This dish is a comforting choice for cold weather, which reminds me of when I was a kid who loved playing in the rain and coming home to a warm meal despite the unceasing scolding for catching a cold. The fun part about this dish is there will always be leftover, where you can reheat it again in 150° F anytime you want and feast with yourself.

Ingredients:

  • 7 cups of water
  • 2 pound pork spare ribs, cut into cube
  • salt and pepper, season it according to your preference
  • 2 tablespoons of fish sauce (Patis)
  • 3 tomatoes, cut into quarter
  • 1 onion, peeled and cut into cube
  • 5 pieces of taro (Gabi), peeled and slice to thick half-round
  • 1 radish, peeled and slice to thick half-round
  • 3 green finger chili, slice it of you like make the soup spicy
  • Vegetables - feel free to add long beans, okra, bok choy, water spinach, and eggplant.
  • 10 pieces of tamarind - preferrably the fresh pods, but you can use powder mixes which you can put 1 and a half

Instructions:

  1. Prepare and Boil Pork
    • Wash and drain pork ribs.
    • In a pot over medium heat, place pork and cover with water. Bring to a boil, skim off any foam that will form.
  2. Simmer Prok with Aromatics
    • Once the broth is clear, add tomatoes, onion, and fish sauce. Lower the heat and simmer to 1 and half hours or wait until the pork is tender. Add water as needed to maintain about 7 cups of water.
  3. Cook Vegetables
    • Add taro and cook for 4 to 6 minutes until tender.
    • Add the chili pepper and radish, sim it for 2 to 3 minutes.
    • Add long beans and cook for 2 minutes.
    • Now add the eggplant and okra and wait for 1 minute.
  4. Seasoning
    • Pour the tamarind powder mix or put the fresh tamarind pods. It depends what are available.
    • Season with salt and pepper.
    • Add bok choy or water spinach and cook for 1 minute
  5. AND YOU'RE DONE!
    • Serve it with love!
